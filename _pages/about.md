Hanxiang Zhang is an Assistant professor in School of Electrical Engineering and Computer Science at University of North Dakota. Beofre joining UND SEECS, he was a postdoctoral research scholar in FAMU-FSU College of Engineering at Florida State University. He earned his Ph.D. degree of Electrical Engineering from department of Electrical and Computer Engineering at Florida State University in 2025. Currently, his research insterest inludes RF/microwave circuits and components design, RF transceiver front-end module, beamforming techniques, wideband phased array system, and real-time RF signal processing, AI-driven microwave circuit design.
