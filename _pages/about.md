Hanxiang Zhang is an Assistant professor in School of Electrical Engineering and Computer Science at University of North Dakota. Beofre joining UND SEECS, he was a postdoctoral research scholar in FAMU-FSU College of Engineering at Florida State University. His research insterest inludes RF/microwave circuits and components design, RF transceiver front-end module, beamforming techniques, wideband phased array system, and real-time RF signal processing, AI-driven microwave circuit design.
